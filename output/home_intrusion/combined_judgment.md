### Key Evidence (Sound + Vision combined)
- **Sound alone**: "Siren detected" — could be a passing ambulance, fire truck, or any emergency vehicle
- **Vision alone**: "A person near a house" — could be a resident, visitor, delivery person, or passerby
- **Combined**: "Siren/alarm sounds coincide with an unidentified person near the residential property, and the person leaves shortly after the alarm triggers — consistent with a triggered security alarm during an intrusion attempt"

### Severity Level
**High** — Security alarm triggered with unidentified person on property

### Recommended Action
1. Review full footage to confirm identity of the individual
2. Check if home security system was triggered
3. Verify all entry points (doors, windows) for signs of tampering
4. If homeowner is away, dispatch security or contact authorities
5. Save footage as evidence
